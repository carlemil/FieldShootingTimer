# Privacy Policy — Fältskyttetimer

**Last updated: 2026-06-08**

Fältskyttetimer collects no personal data, transmits no data over the network,
and contains no third-party SDKs, analytics, advertising, or tracking.

## Data the app stores on your device

The app persists two pieces of information locally on your device so they
survive between launches:

- The configured fire-phase duration (seconds).
- The positions of any markers ("ticks") you have placed on the timer.

These are written to the app's private storage area and are never read by, or
sent to, anyone outside the app. Uninstalling the app removes them.

## Permissions

The app requests no system permissions (no microphone, no camera, no location,
no contacts, no network).

## Audio and haptics

The app plays pre-recorded Swedish voice cues from files bundled with the app.
Audio respects the device's silent switch.
